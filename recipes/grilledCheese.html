Gilled Cheese

Description

Grilled Cheese is one of the most loved dishes around the world. It can be made very easily at home if you have all the ingredients. In this article, you will learn how to make a grilled cheese easily at home.

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Bread
  • American Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Take two slices of bread and put two slices of american cheese and some mozzarella on one slice and cover with other.
  2. Heat butter in pan and grill the bread until crispy. Enjoy!
